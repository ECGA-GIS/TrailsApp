If you choose to use our service, then you agree to the voluntary and temporary use of location information. We will not use or share your information with anyone.

Information You Provide to Us

Greenbelt does not keep any personal information about you or your use of this application unless submitted by the user in the 'Report Trail Issue' tool. If you choose to use the 'Report Trail Issue' functionality in this application, the application will record your location using your device's GPS and any attached content, images or data, and submit it to Greenbelt staff. This data will only be used by Greenbelt staff for land management purposes and will not be released to the public.
